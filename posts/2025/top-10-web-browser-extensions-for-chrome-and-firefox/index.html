    What is an Extension/Add-on?

    A browser extension, often referred to as an add-on, is a small software program designed to enhance and customize the functionality of web browsers such as Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari, or Microsoft Edge. These extensions can add new features, modify existing ones, or even block unwanted content.

    Whether it’s blocking ads for a cleaner browsing experience, managing passwords securely, or accelerating downloads, browser extensions offer users a way to tailor their browsing experience to their needs and preferences. With thousands of extensions available, users have the flexibility to choose and install the ones that best suit their requirements, making their online activities more efficient, secure, and enjoyable.

    2. Popup Blocker (strict)

    “Popup Blocker (strict)” is an extension that provides a reliable popup blocker for your browser. Whenever a website attempts to open a popup, it will be automatically blocked, while a notification containing the popup’s link will appear in the upper right corner of the browser window. The primary purpose of this extension is to prevent the opening of new tabs or windows, rather than blocking floating in-page popups.

    • Popup Blocker (strict)

    • Popup Blocker (strict)

    •by Jeremy Schomery



    3. uBlock Origin

    “uBlock Origin” is a content blocker that efficiently blocks ads, trackers, and popups. It uses default filter lists like EasyList and EasyPrivacy. Users can customize their blocking and it’s free and open-source, supported y community contributions.

    • uBlock Origin

    • uBlock Origin

    •by Raymond Hill



    4. Dark Reader

    “Dark Reader” is a popular extension that enables dark mode for websites. It’s easy on the eyes and reduces eye strain, especially during nighttime browsing. Default shortcut for dark reader is usually set to Shift+Alt+D but you can change it in the settings to suit your needs.

    • Dark Reader

    • Dark Reader

    •by Dark Reader Ltd



    5. Return YouTube Dislike

    “Return YouTube Dislike” restores the ability to see dislikes on YouTube. Starting December 13th 2021, YouTube removed the ability to see dislikes from their API.

    • Return YouTube Dislike

    • Return YouTube Dislike

    •by Dmitry Selivanov



    6. Video Speed Controller

    “Video Speed Controller” is a browser extension that enhances HTML5 video playback by allowing users to adjust playback speed, rewind, and control videos more effectively. It provides on-screen indicators and keyboard shortcuts for speed adjustments, making video viewing more optimized and customizable.

    S - decrease playback speed.
    D - increase playback speed.
    R - reset playback speed.
    Z - rewind video by 10 seconds.
    X - advance video by 10 seconds.
    V - show/hide controller.

    • Video Speed Controller

    • Video Speed Controller

    •by codebicycle



    7. Extensity

    “Extensity” is a convenient tool for Google Chrome users to quickly enable or disable browser extensions. It helps keep your toolbar clean by allowing you to activate extensions only when needed, ensuring a lean and fast browsing experience. With features like profiles for easy switching and Chrome Cloud Storage support for syncing across devices, Extensity is an ideal companion for managing your Chrome extensions efficiently.

    • Extensity

    •by sergiokas



    8. Bitwarden Password Manager

    “Bitwarden” is a top-rated password manager recognized by leading tech publications like PCMag and WIRED. It offers end-to-end encrypted storage for your passwords, ensuring your digital life remains secure against data breaches. With features like advanced 2FA, secure data sharing, and global translations, Bitwarden provides versatile protection across all your devices.

    • Bitwarden

    • Bitwarden

    “eJOY AI Dictionary” extension revolutionizes your online learning by offering real-time translation, bilingual subtitles, and an AI-powered dictionary for instant definitions. Seamlessly integrated with your browser, it transforms any English content into an accessible learning resource. Elevate your learning journey with organized notes, interactive games, and smart review algorithms, making English mastery joyful and efficient.

    10. Clear Browsing Data

    “Clear Browsing Data” is a browser extension that lets you quickly delete cookies, history, and cache from your browser toolbar with a single click. It supports various data types like cookies, history, and saved passwords, which can be customized.
